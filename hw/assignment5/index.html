      Hi, my name is Natalia Diaz. Welcome to my cite about traveling through Venice, Italy. I stayed in Venice for two days, while on vacation a couple years ago. It was a wonderful, yet all too brief stay. However, the pictures above capture some of the best views and locations I had the priviledge to experience. While walking through the city center I admired the beautiful architecture, came across some eclectic markets, and eat some authentic Italian pasta. In the evening I scratched a major item off my bucket list; tour the canals of Venice via gondola. Super tourist-y, I know, but it's a must-do experience. On day two of my stay I visited the Piazza San Marco, where I basked in the marvels of St. Mark's Basilica, and the Clock Tower. The only thing I would've changed about this trip was the duration of my stay-- it definitely could have been extended a few days.
